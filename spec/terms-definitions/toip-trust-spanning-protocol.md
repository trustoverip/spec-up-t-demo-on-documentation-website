[[def: ToIP Trust Spanning Protocol, TSP]]
~ The ToIP Trust Spanning Protocol (TSP) is the ToIP Layer 2 protocol for [[ref: verifiable messaging]] that implements the [[ref: trust spanning layer]] of the [[ref: ToIP stack]].Â  The TSP enables [[ref: actors]] in different digital [[ref: trust domains]] to interact in a similar way to how the Internet Protocol (IP) enables devices on different local area networks to exchange data.

~ Mental model: [[ref: hourglass model,]] see the [Design Principles for the ToIP Stack](https://trustoverip.org/permalink/Design-Principles-for-the-ToIP-Stack-V1.0-2022-11-17.pdf).
